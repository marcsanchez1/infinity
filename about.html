About Us

Founded in 2016

With over 20 years of experience, Inifinity Metal and Marble, LLC. was founded to provide a level of service and quality to our client matched by no one in the industry. We strive to always maintain a high level of professionalism while also creating a relationship that builds confidence in our team with 100% satisfaction.

Inifinity Metal and Marble, LLC is fully licensed and insured. Family owned and operated, we have been providing a broad range of high quality restoration and maintenance services to commercial, residential, retail and hospitality facilities throughout the tri-state area. Our restoration technicians dedicate their craftmanship to every project.
